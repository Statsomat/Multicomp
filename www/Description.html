Description

The app performs Multiple Comparisons Procedures with a Control, in particular the Dunnet test for linear models with at least one factor (categorical predictor) of interest. Annotated output tables, graphics and a theoretical background are included where appropiate. Large parts of the app where developed by students at the Faculty of the Mathematics and Technics at the University of Applied Sciences Koblenz, Germany during a summer semester 2021 course. The app is open source, check the GitHub repository.
